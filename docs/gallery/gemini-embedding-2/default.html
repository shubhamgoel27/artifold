Gemini Embedding 2

A Native Multimodal Embedding Model from Gemini

arXiv: 2605.27295

Submitted: May 26, 2026

Authors: Madhuri Shanbhogue, Zhe Li, Shanfeng Zhang, Gustavo Hernández Ábrego, Shih-Cheng Huang, et al. (88 total)

Abstract

Gemini Embedding 2 is a native multimodal embedding model built on top of Gemini that embeds video, audio, image, and text into a single unified representation space. Trained with large-scale contrastive learning in a multi-task, multi-stage setup, it achieves state-of-the-art results on a wide range of retrieval benchmarks and demonstrates strong zero-shot transfer to specialized domains such as astronomy, bioscience, fine arts, and culinary.

Key Contributions

  • Unified multimodal embedding space: A single model that encodes video, audio, image, and text into the same vector space, enabling cross-modal retrieval out of the box.
  • Built on Gemini: Leverages the Gemini foundation model, inheriting its native multimodal understanding rather than bolting modalities on after the fact.
  • Multi-task, multi-stage training: Uses large-scale contrastive learning across a diverse mix of tasks and stages to produce robust, general-purpose embeddings.
  • State-of-the-art retrieval performance: Top results across image-text, video-text, multilingual, and code retrieval benchmarks.
  • Strong zero-shot domain transfer: Generalizes well to specialized domains (astronomy, bioscience, fine arts, culinary) without domain-specific fine-tuning.
