Field Notes · No. 14 · Coffee

Ten SF coffee bars,
ranked honestly.

San Francisco May 2026 10 shops · 3 axes

A working tier list of pour-over rooms in the city, judged on three things and three things only: what comes out of the V60, whether you want to sit there, and whether you can. No latte art scoring, no pastry detours, no rewards for marketing.

S
Worth a crosstown trip. All three axes land.
A

B
Solid daily driver. Strong somewhere, soft elsewhere.

Saint Frank
Russian Hill · Polk & Vallejo
The most consistent V60 in the city, full stop.
Pour-over
5.0
Tight single-origin program, baristas weigh and time every cup.
Ambience
4.5
White subway tile, marble bar, calm even at peak. Sunlit afternoons.
Seating
4.0
Bar stools plus a back room. Usually a seat by 10am on weekdays.
Linea Caffe
Mission · 18th near Linda
Andrew Barnett's hand-poured cups, no laptops, no fuss.
Pour-over
5.0
Ex-Ecco roaster pulling extraordinary Ethiopians. Genuinely a destination cup.
Ambience
4.5
Tiny, bright, conversational. Forced presence — phones go down.
Seating
4.0
Sidewalk bench + a handful of stools. Turnover is fast because nobody camps.
