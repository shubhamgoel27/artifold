Method

Each room visited at least three times across different days. Scores are blind-rated against an internal rubric — pour-over weighted 1.2×, ambience 1.0×, seating 0.8× — then bucketed. Composite cutoffs at 8.5 (S), 7.5 (A), 6.5 (B).
