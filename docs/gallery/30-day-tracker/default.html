How to progress

  • Add 1 rep per set each session until you hit the top of the range.
  • Add 2.5-5 lb when you complete all sets at the top rep range with 1-2 RIR.
  • Form first: if reps look ugly, repeat the day at the same weight.
  • Rest: 60-90 sec on isolation, 90-120 sec on squats/RDLs.
  • Tempo: 2 sec down, brief pause, controlled up.

Daily checklist

  • 5 min warm-up (bike or jumping jacks)
  • 0.7-1 g protein per lb bodyweight
  • 2-3 L water
  • 7-9 hrs sleep
  • Log weights used in margin
Print on US Letter or A4 (landscape recommended). Replace dumbbell weights with whatever pair you own — the rep targets matter more than the load.
