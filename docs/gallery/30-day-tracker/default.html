How to use this tracker

  • Warm up 5 minutes: light cardio + arm circles + bodyweight squats before every workout.
  • If a set feels easier than RPE 7, add 1-2 reps or 2.5-5 lb next session.
  • If form breaks down, stop the set. Quality beats quantity every time.
  • Rest days are non-negotiable — muscle grows when you recover.
  • Eat ~0.7 g protein per lb bodyweight and sleep 7-9 hours nightly.
  • Missed a day? Just resume on the next scheduled day — don't double up.
