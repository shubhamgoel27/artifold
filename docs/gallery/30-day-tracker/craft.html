PROGRAM / BEG-30

Thirty days. Five lifts.
Add weight every session.

BLOCK 01 · BEGINNER
3× / WEEK · FULL-BODY
EST. 55 MIN / SESSION

Show up. Three days a week. Add 5 lb to lower-body lifts, 2.5 lb to upper-body lifts — every time the previous session went clean.

How to use this sheet. Print one page per week. Write the bar weight into the empty cell next to each lift. Tick the box at the bottom of the day when every set is done at full quality.

Skipped a day? Don't double up. Pick up where you left off. The dates slide, the order doesn't.

                                    § 03

                                    Form, in five lines per lift

                                    Read before every session
                                    SQ · BACK SQUAT

                                    Sit between your hips.

                                    1. Bar on rear delts, not on neck.
                                    2. Brace. Big breath, hold it down.
                                    3. Break at hips and knees together.
                                    4. Crease of hip below top of knee.
                                    5. Drive the floor away. Exhale at top.
                                    BP · BENCH PRESS

                                    Press the bar, not the ceiling.

                                    1. Shoulder blades pinched and pulled down.
                                    2. Feet flat. Light arch, glutes on bench.
                                    3. Bar to lower sternum, elbows ~ 60°.
                                    4. Touch, pause one beat, press.
                                    5. No bouncing. No half-reps.
                                    DL · DEADLIFT

                                    One set. Make it perfect.

                                    1. Bar over mid-foot. Shins almost touch.
                                    2. Hips high enough that arms hang vertical.
                                    3. Brace, drag the bar up your shins.
                                    4. Lock knees and hips at the same time.
                                    5. Lower with control. Reset every rep.
                                    OHP · OVERHEAD PRESS

                                    Push your head through.

                                    1. Bar on front delts, elbows just ahead.
                                    2. Squeeze glutes. Don't lean back.
                                    3. Press in a straight line over crown.
                                    4. At lockout, head moves under bar.
                                    5. If the bar stalls, brace harder.
                                    BR · BARBELL ROW

                                    Row to the belly, not the chest.

                                    1. Hinge to ~ 45°. Flat back.
                                    2. Bar starts on the floor every rep.
                                    3. Pull elbows back and down.
                                    4. Touch lower ribs. Pause.
                                    5. Don't let the torso bounce.
                                    RECOVERY

                                    The lifts work because you sleep.

                                    1. Sleep 7–9 hrs. Non-negotiable.
                                    2. Eat 1g protein per lb bodyweight.
                                    3. Walk 30 min on rest days.
                                    4. If a set feels wrong, stop the set.
                                    5. If a lift stalls 2× in a row, deload 10%.
