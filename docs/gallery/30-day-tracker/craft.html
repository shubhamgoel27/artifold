Protocol

House rules

Warm-up every session: 5 min bike/row, then 2–3 ramp sets per main lift (empty bar → 50% → 75% of the day's working load). The numbers in the ledger are working sets, not ramp sets.

RPE is rated after the last set: 6 = three reps in reserve, 7 = two, 8 = one, 9 = could grind one more. If all working sets cross RPE 9 on the same lift, hold load for the next session.

Eat & sleep

  • Protein: 0.8 g per lb bodyweight, daily.
  • Pre-lift: a real meal, 90 min out.
  • Sleep target: 7.5 h+ on training nights.
  • Water: ~½ oz per lb bodyweight.

Red flags · stop & reset

  • Sharp joint pain (not soreness).
  • Form failure on rep 1 of a working set.
  • Two consecutive failed sessions on the same lift.
  • Resting HR up 10+ bpm for 3 days.
